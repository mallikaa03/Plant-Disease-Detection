POTATO PLANT DISEASE DETECTION

Potato crop is prone to conditions like EarlyBlight and Late Blight and not being able to detect them leads to a poor production and hence also incurs major losses to farmers.

This model takes an image of the potato plant leaf as an input and detects if the plant has the above mentioned conditons or not with high confidence rate. I have used convolutional neural networks in this model.
